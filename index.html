Artist's Statement

I work in several mediums, traditional and non-traditional. I learned my art forms from a variety of sources, including classes, practice, and searching for tips online. I have been working in digital and web art forms in earnest only since the start of this semester. I have been doing traditional drawing and sculpture in some capacity for many years. I am motivated to make art by my liking for doing so. I also find it occasionally cathartic to make art. There is no common thread through my work, as much of it is made with the mentality that it will be a solo piece. Art is important to me because it serves not only as entertainment, but a way to express things not easily expressed otherwise and a way to forge empathy in some cases.
